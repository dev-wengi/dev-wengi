# AI Email Assistant

Category: Advanced

## End of 30-Day Goal

This AI Email Assistant should help users draft, revise, and improve emails with clear, context-aware suggestions.

## Expected Features

- Accept a draft or prompt for an email.
- Generate suggested wording, subject lines, and tone improvements.
- Offer alternate phrasing and clarity edits.
- A simple interface for reviewing email drafts.

## Success Criteria

- Users can write better emails faster.
- Suggestions are relevant and easy to apply.
- The app feels like a practical writing assistant.
