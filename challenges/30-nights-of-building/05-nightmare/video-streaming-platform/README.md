# Video Streaming Platform

Category: Nightmare

## End of 30-Day Goal

This Video Streaming Platform should let users browse media, play videos, and manage streaming content with a modern UX.

## Expected Features

- Display a video library with categories.
- Play selected videos in an embedded player.
- Show video details and recommendations.
- Responsive viewing experience.
- Clean navigation for browsing streaming content.

## Success Criteria

- Users can find and watch videos easily.
- The platform feels polished and enjoyable.
- The app demonstrates a functional streaming interface.
