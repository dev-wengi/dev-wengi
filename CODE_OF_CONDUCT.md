# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for all contributors, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to creating a positive environment include:

- **Being respectful**: Use welcoming and inclusive language. Be courteous in all interactions, both online and offline.
- **Being collaborative**: Work constructively with others. Share knowledge and help fellow contributors succeed.
- **Being constructive**: Provide thoughtful feedback and criticism. Focus on the work, not the person.
- **Being patient**: Understand that people have different backgrounds and expertise levels. Take time to explain and learn.
- **Assuming good intent**: Give others the benefit of the doubt. Miscommunications happen—clarify rather than accuse.

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- **Harassment**: Comments about someone's appearance, identity, or abilities; unwanted sexual attention; deliberate intimidation; or stalking.
- **Discrimination**: Excluding or marginalizing individuals based on protected characteristics or personal identity.
- **Disruptive behavior**: Trolling, insulting comments, personal attacks, or derailing conversations.
- **Abusive language**: Slurs, profanity used to demean, or dehumanizing language.
- **Unwelcome advances**: Any form of sexual or romantic advances that are not reciprocated or welcome.
- **Threats or violence**: Any threats of violence, harm, or illegal action.
- **Plagiarism**: Claiming credit for work that is not your own.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all community spaces, including:

- GitHub repositories and discussions
- Project issues and pull requests
- Commit messages and code reviews
- Community forums, chat channels, and social media
- In-person events organized by the project

## Enforcement

### Reporting Violations

If you witness or experience a violation of this Code of Conduct, please report it by:

1. **Direct communication**: If comfortable, address the issue directly with the person involved.
2. **Project maintainers**: Contact the repository maintainers via email or private message on GitHub.
3. **Creating an issue**: File a private security advisory or confidential issue (if the platform supports it).

Please include:
- A description of what happened
- Who was involved
- When and where it occurred
- Any relevant context or evidence

### Investigation and Response

Upon receiving a report, the project maintainers will:

1. Acknowledge receipt of your report within 48 hours
2. Investigate the matter fairly and thoroughly
3. Maintain confidentiality of the reporter(s) as much as possible
4. Take appropriate action based on findings

Possible actions include:

- A warning or request for behavioral change
- Temporary or permanent removal of access to project spaces
- Public or private apology
- Escalation to appropriate authorities if necessary

## Consequences

Community members who violate this Code of Conduct may face consequences including:

- A warning and request to modify behavior
- Temporary suspension from project participation
- Permanent ban from the project community
- Removal of contributed content
- Legal action if applicable

## Our Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for:

- Clarifying standards of acceptable behavior
- Taking appropriate action in response to unacceptable behavior
- Removing, editing, or rejecting comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions that violate this Code of Conduct
- Being fair, impartial, and transparent in enforcement

Maintainers who do not follow or enforce the Code of Conduct in good faith may face temporary or permanent removal from their role.

## Questions or Concerns?

If you have questions about this Code of Conduct or need clarification on any part of it, please reach out to the project maintainers. We're here to help create a positive community experience for everyone.
